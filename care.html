How To Take Care 🪴

  • ☀️ Give 4–6 hours of sunlight daily
  • 💧 Keep soil moist with distilled water
  • 🚫 Avoid normal fertilizers
  • 🐜 Feed insects occasionally
  • ❄️ Allow winter dormancy

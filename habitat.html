Where Does It Grow? 🌿

Venus Flytraps grow naturally in wetlands and swampy regions 💧. These places have poor soil, so the plant evolved to eat insects.

The plant loves:

  • ☀️ Bright sunlight
  • 💧 Moist soil
  • 🌡️ Warm temperatures
  • 🍃 Humid environment

Sadly, habitat destruction threatens wild Venus Flytraps 😢. Conservation efforts are helping protect them.

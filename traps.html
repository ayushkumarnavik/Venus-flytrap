How Does The Trap Work? ⚡

The Venus Flytrap has tiny trigger hairs inside its leaves. When an insect touches two hairs within about 20 seconds, the trap snaps shut rapidly 😲.

Steps of Hunting 🐜

  1. 🪰 Insect lands on the leaf
  2. 👆 Trigger hairs are touched
  3. ⚡ Trap closes instantly
  4. 🍽️ Plant digests the insect
  5. 🌿 Trap opens again after digestion

This incredible movement makes the Venus Flytrap one of the fastest-moving plants in the world 🌎✨.

Scientific Information 🔬

  • Scientific Name: Dionaea muscipula
  • Family: Droseraceae
  • Type: Carnivorous Plant
  • Native Region: North & South Carolina

Venus Flytraps are famous for their incredible hunting ability 🪰. Their leaves contain sensitive trigger hairs. When insects touch these hairs, the trap closes rapidly!

This amazing plant teaches us how intelligent nature can be 🌱✨.
